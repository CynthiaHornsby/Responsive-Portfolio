About Me

Good day, and welcome to my humble abode among the internet city. As a determined student of the knowledge life offers, I take an interest in a variety of disiplines from art, to history, and recent adventures in coding. Finding that each facet adds and builds upon one another, it is this addition that I have a passion in chasing; no detail is too great, nor too small, to be helpful. With this mantra in heart I have taken up arms and march into the world of art and code, set to produce functioning visuals for all- with a world of exploration to tackle along the way, of course.
